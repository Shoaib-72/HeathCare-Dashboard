🫁 Smoking Health Risk Analysis — Power BI Dashboard
📌 Short Description / Purpose
An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes the health impact of smoking across a patient population of 2,500+. It helps healthcare analysts, researchers, and public health professionals explore smoking behavior patterns, organ damage indicators, gender-based smoking trends, and cardiovascular risk factors — all in one unified view.

🧰 Tech Stack

Visualization: Microsoft Power BI Desktop
Data Processing: Power Query (ETL)
Calculations: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Format: .pbix


📂 Data Source

Healthcare/clinical patient dataset containing demographic info (age, gender, BMI), smoking history (status, duration, cigarettes per day), and health indicators (cholesterol, hypertension levels)
(Likely sourced from Kaggle's Smoking & Health dataset or a synthetic clinical dataset)

🧩 Business Problem
Smoking is the leading cause of preventable death globally, yet patient-level data on its cumulative health impact is rarely visualized in an accessible format. Healthcare providers and analysts need a tool to quickly identify which patient segments are at highest risk for organ damage, hypertension, and cardiovascular disease — based on smoking history and demographics.

❓ Key Questions Answered

What percentage of patients are current, former, or never-smokers?
How does smoking duration and daily cigarette intake vary across age groups?
Is there a gender difference in smoking status distribution?
Which age groups face the highest cholesterol and hypertension risk?
What do damaged organs look like compared to healthy ones across smoking categories?
What is the average BMI and age profile of the patient population?
